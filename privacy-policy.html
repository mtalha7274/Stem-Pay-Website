Privacy Policy

1. Data We Don’t Collect: We never request or store your private keys, seed phrases, passwords, or other sensitive credentials. All wallet data remains encrypted and local to your device.

2. Transaction Data: We collect only the minimum on-chain transaction details (addresses, amounts, timestamps) strictly to facilitate and audit lottery entries.

3. Analytics & Cookies: We do not use third-party analytics or tracking cookies. Any site-usage metrics are gathered client-side and never shared or stored on our servers.

4. Third-Party Links: Links to external sites (e.g. PolygonScan, Chainlink) are provided for convenience. We do not control those sites’ privacy practices.

5. Data Security: While we employ industry-standard encryption for any transient data, loss or compromise of your keys remains your responsibility. Always back up your wallet.

6. Policy Updates: This policy may be revised—last updated April 28, 2025. Any changes will be posted here with a new date; please review periodically.
